What is Braid?

Braid is a Client library for a portion of the IHE (Integrating the Healthcare Enterprise) transactions.

This library specifically focuses on PIX, XDS.a & XDS.b, ATNA, XDS-MS (Referral & Discharge Summary), EDR, BPPC, XDS-SD profiles.

IHE is organized into multiple Domains which each address problems in one area of healthcare. Braid focused on two domains.

IT Infrastructure (ITI)

• Cross-Enterprise Document Sharing of Scanned Documents (Content Creation and View)
• Cross-Enterprise Clinical Documents Share (XDS.a) (XDS.a Source & XDS.a Consumer)
• Cross-Enterprise Clinical Documents Share (XDS.b) (XDS.b Source & XDS.b Consumer)
• Patient Identifier Cross-referencing for MPI (PIX v2 Source & PIX v2 Consumer)
• Patient Demographics Query (PDQ v2 Consumer)
• Audit Trail and Node Authentication (ATNA)
• Consistent Time (CT)

Patient Care Coordination (PCC)

• Emergency Department Referral (ED Referral) (Content Creation and View)
• Cross-Enterprise Sharing of Medical Summaries (Content Creation and View)
• Referral Summary
• Discharge Summary
• Basic Patient Privacy Consents (BPPC) (BPPC Content & Enforcement)

Braid Client libraries are successfully tested with other vendors in Connectathon-2008 event.
